What's Product Design?

Product design is the process of creating and developing a new product, considering various aspects such as aesthetics, functionality, ergonomics, and usability. It involves a multidisciplinary approach that combines elements of engineering, art, and business to meet the needs and preferences of users. The goal of product design is to create innovative and marketable solutions that enhance the user experience and address specific problems or challenges. Designers typically follow a systematic process that includes research, conceptualization, prototyping, and testing to bring a product from idea to realization. Effective product design plays a crucial role in the success of a product in the market.

What is Graphic Design?

Graphic design is a creative and strategic process that involves visual communication and problem-solving using typography, imagery, color, and form. Graphic designers utilize various elements and principles to convey a message or information visually. This field is broad and encompasses a wide range of mediums, including print, digital, and multimedia.

Core Principles of Graphic Design

Graphic design is a creative and visual communication discipline that involves combining text, images, symbols, and other visual elements to convey information and messages to a target audience. It is a form of visual communication that seeks to solve problems and communicate ideas through the use of typography, color, imagery, and layout. Graphic designers work across various media, including print, digital, and multimedia platforms, to create visually compelling and effective designs.
